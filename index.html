RV University


Multi-cuisine food court “Mingo’s” – the multi-cuisine food court located in the RV University campus is designed to cater to over a 1000 students, faculty and staff. This spacious and well-ventilated cafeteria is spread over 500 square meters. Food is prepared in a modern kitchen where it is steam cooked. The washed dishes are sterilized for hygienic serving. Apart from the main cafeteria, the campus also hosts a mini-food joint that offers quick and ready-to-eat snacks to satiate the hunger of its customers. This is conveniently located at the centre of the campus.
